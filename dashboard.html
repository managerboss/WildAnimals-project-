African Wild Animals

Learn about Africa's major wild animals, their features, and fascinating habits

Lion

Lion Characteristics

  • The lion is a large animal of the cat family (Felidae)
  • They have colors ranging from brown to yellow and males can have a mane
  • Males weigh between 120-250 kg and females 120-182 kg
  • They have large sharp teeth for hunting
  • They can run at speeds up to 80 km/h for short distances

Leopard

Leopard Characteristics

  • Leopard has skin covered with small spots
  • They have colors ranging from brown or yellow with black spots
  • They usually weigh between 30-90 kg
  • They have a long body and strong legs
  • They can climb trees very well

Antelope

Antelope Characteristics

  • Antelope is a herbivore that feeds on grasses
  • They are fast runners and can reach speeds up to 80 km/h
  • They have light brown fur with white markings
  • Antelopes have long slender legs and necks
  • They live in herds for protection

Elephant

Elephant Characteristics

  • Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth
  • They have large ears and long trunks used for grabbing food and water
  • They can weigh up to 6000 kg
  • Elephants live in herds and have strong social bonds
  • They are herbivores and eat grass, leaves, and bark

Giraffe

Giraffe Characteristics

  • Giraffes are the tallest land animals
  • They have long necks that help them reach leaves on tall trees
  • They weigh between 800-1200 kg
  • They have spotted patterns on their fur which vary between individuals
  • Giraffes can run at speeds up to 60 km/h
